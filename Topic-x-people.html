X2: X-Men United' Arrives on Disney+ in October

After you’re done watching 2000’s X-Men on Disney+, you can move right onto X2! The sequel to the blockbuster movie lands on the streaming service in just a few short days on October 9, starting the month off with a snikt! Picking up after the events of the first movie (which you can watch on Disney+ right now), in X2: X-Men United, an anti-mutant "black ops" specialist abducts Charles Xavier, imprisons his students and steals Cerebro. This leads the fugitive X-Men and newcomer Night Crawler join forces with Magneto to rescue their brethren and forestall a holocaust. That’s not all that’s landing on Disney+ this month! If you’re looking to dive into some animated Super Heroes, Marvel’s Iron Man & Captain America: Heroes United arrives on the platform on October 16. After Red Skull and Taskmaster devise a plan to get rid of Captain America, he teams up with Iron Man to stop them before they get their hands on advanced Stark technology. And joining Seasons 1 - 3, the new episodes of Marvel Superhero Adventures arrive on October 23! Looking for more? Find everything coming to Disney+ this month in the video below!
